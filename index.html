Coffee sorted
Individual half & half creamer cups are fully shelf-stable. Grab a box at Costco or Amazon — ~1g carb per cup.
Tuna = instant lunch
Tear open the tuna pouch, squeeze in the mayo packet, eat with a fork. StarKist flavored pouches (lemon pepper, ranch) are surprisingly good.
PB&J smart swap
Use natural peanut butter (just peanuts + salt) and a low-sugar jelly to keep PB&J day carbs on the lower end.
Mayo packets
Hellmann's single-serve packets are on Amazon in bulk. Toss a few in your bag Sunday night and forget about it.
Pre-portion almonds
Weigh out 1 oz servings (~23 nuts) into small zip bags on Sunday. Saves you from snack math all week.
Premier vs. Fairlife
Both work great. Premier Protein is cheaper; Fairlife Core Power has 42g protein. Both are ~3g net carbs.
Hydration
Low carb flushes electrolytes. Add a pinch of salt to water, or try LMNT / Liquid IV zero sugar packets if you feel sluggish.
Morning fridge routine
Each morning, drop eggs + string cheese + cottage cheese (if that day) into a bag. 10-second habit that saves the whole day.
